Please discuss as a group what information you require to make informed decisions about prioritization and allocation of limited reconstruction funding. Based on discussions in the course and readings, what information is critical to addressing both short and long-term needs, as well as confronting continued conflict stressors and potential relapses of conflict?

Your group will be able to request 5 reports from the following list of potential reports that can be prepared by the existing UN agencies on the ground to help with your decision making process.

  1. Water Resources Management - This report discusses the availability of water resources in the country, focusing on supply, demand, and climate characteristics. It also discusses existing infrastructure.
  2. Agricultural Practices - This report provides an summary of the agricultural sector in Nimpala, discussing production statistics, role in the economy and potential role in long-term development.
  3. Economic Profile - This report gives a basic overview of the economy in Nimpala. It details production by macro economic trends, history of economic growth, and infrastructure investment.
  4. Humanitarian Situation - This report gives an overview of the concerns about health, basic services, and population displacement due to the recent conflict.
  5. Political Vulnerability Profile - This report discusses the national political situation, current security situation, and post-conflict stabilization efforts.
  6. Environmental Health - Brief overview of the health sector and major concerns of post-crisis health issues, specifically related to the environment.
  7. Natural Disasters -Overview of the risks to flooding and drought and details of historic climate trends.
  8. Population - Description of the population dynamics within the country, distribution and density as well as broader population growth and displacement estimates.
  9. Wildlife - This details conservation needs in the country, including remaining national parks, wildlife, and information on threats generated by the conflict to critical wildlife species.
  10. Environmental Governance - This report details the existing institutions that role of environmental legislation, governance and management.
  11. Forest Resources - This report summarizes the remaining timber and forest resources and discusses the economic value.

Please think carefully about which reports from the following list you would like to request and why that information is critical to your analysis. Please be prepared to discuss your selection briefly in class and write memo with your team's rationale for purchasing each report.
