Please discuss as a group what information you require to make informed decisions about prioritization and allocation of limited reconstruction funding. Based on discussions in the course and readings, what information is critical to addressing both short and long-term needs, as well as confronting continued conflict stressors and potential relapses of conflict?

Your group will be able to request 5 reports from the following list of potential reports that can be prepared by the existing UN agencies on the ground to help with your decision making process.
